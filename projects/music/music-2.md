β-KETIN (pronounced "beta-KETIN") is a music duo formed in 2024 realizing itself in live improvised electronic music performances.
The music can be characaterised as a mix between ambient, drone, and techno.
Setups and instruments change from performance to performance.
So far, β-KETIN has performed in the Czech Republic and in Poland.
